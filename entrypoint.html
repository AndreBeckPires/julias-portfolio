Premier Entry Point Integration

Improving how business customers access Dell Premier by integrating a clear entry point into Dell.com’s global masthead — enhancing visibility and reducing navigation friction.
Role Product Designer
Platforms Web
Areas UX Strategy, Interaction Design, Navigation Design

Background

Dell Premier is a personalized procurement platform for business customers. The challenge was to make Premier more discoverable without disrupting the existing navigation structure or overwhelming the user experience.

Key issues

Before this update, many users struggled to locate the Premier entry point from Dell.com’s main navigation, which created unnecessary friction and impacted their ability to complete purchases efficiently.

Approach

Our goal was to improve findability while maintaining a clean, intuitive masthead. To achieve this, the team:
  • Analyzed navigation patterns and reviewed customer feedback to validate the problem.
  • Explored multiple design options for integrating the Premier link into the masthead.
  • Created wireframes and high-fidelity mockups aligned with Dell’s design system.
  • Collaborated with developers and stakeholders to ensure seamless implementation.
  • Conducted usability testing to confirm improved visibility and clarity.
The final design introduced a dedicated Premier entry point in the global masthead, using clear labeling and visual hierarchy to guide business customers effectively.

Impact

Results from the live site implementation, driving increased Premier awareness and organic B2B lead growth.

Organic clicks +48.6×
Page visits +70%
New accounts +2.8x
